About LeafAI

LeafAI is a modern web application designed to help farmers, students, and plant enthusiasts quickly identify plant diseases from leaf images using deep learning and the Gemini AI API. The project provides actionable advice and routines for plant recovery, and features an AI-powered chatbot for all your plant health questions.

Features

  • Instant plant disease detection from leaf images
  • 7-day recovery routines tailored to each disease
  • Gemini AI-powered chatbot for Q&A
  • Beautiful, responsive, and user-friendly interface
  • Project details and credits
